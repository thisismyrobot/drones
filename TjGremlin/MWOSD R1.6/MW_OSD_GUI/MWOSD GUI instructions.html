MWOSD GUI configurator - MWOSD 1.7 onwards

The GUI for configuring MWOSD is available for a nominal fee at the google chrome store.

Why is the OSD source code free, but the GUI not?
- The OS vendors announced a while ago their intention to withdraw support for some functions used by the legacy "Processing" based GUI. To free up development time for the OSD and have a more up to date, reliable and faster operating GUI, the MWOSD repository owner took the decision to work with a developer who would create a suitable replacement 1.6 GUI FOC and provide a supported one in the future at minimal cost. The GUI configurator is built from the ground up purely for MWOSD and will not be open sourced, however the files are available to the MWOSD repository owner to inpsect and make changes if necessary.

MWOSD GUI configurator - MWOSD 1.6 ***FREE*** version

A free version of the MWOSD GUI configurator has kindly been provided by its developer for MWOSD R1.6

The free version is available in the MWOSD 1.6 download pack.

Manually installing the R1.6 free version of MWOSD GUI configurator in Chrome
1. Click on the three lines shown on the top-right corner in your browser, which will open a menu for you. Click on “Settings.” You will be redirected to your browser’s settings page.

2. Once on the Settings page, click on the “Extensions” link given in the left-hand side of your screen. It will take you to the extensions page that shows all the extensions you have installed in your browser.

3. On the extensions page, you should see a checkbox on the top saying “Developer mode.” Click on it.

4. Drag the MW_OSD_GUI.CRX file and drop it onto the extensions page.

5. Once you have dropped the file, you may be prompted to add the extension to your browser. Click on “Add” / "Continue" to manually install the extension.

6. Follow the prompts to install it
